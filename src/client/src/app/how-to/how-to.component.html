Instructions
  • 🏆 The objective of the game is to be the player with the most points at the end
  • 🕒 The game is divided into rounds based on the number of characters in the chosen set
  • 👥 Each player gets a turn and for each turn gets to choose a letter/character to be played
  • 📝 When a letter is selected, all players have to write in words starting with the chosen character corresponding to the columns listed
  • ⏱️ Speed is of the essence as you need to fill your entries in before the timer runs out or the round author ends the round
  • 👨‍🏫 After each round, the player whose turn it is gets to grade all entries
  • 🎉 Unique, valid entries get 10 points, while duplicate entries get 5 points
